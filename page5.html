Gu Kaizhi

Gu Kaizhi (345 A.D-406 A.D.) was a Chinese painter, poet and politician. He often painted portraits.

Hokusai

Hokusai (1760-1849) was a Japanese painter and printmaker. His most famous painting is The Great Wave Off Kanagawa.
