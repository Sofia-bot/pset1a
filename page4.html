William Blake

William Blake (1757-1827) was an English poet and painter. His most famous paintings include The Ancient of Days and The Ghost of a Flea.

Caspar David Friedrich

Caspar David Friedrich (1774-1840) was a German painter. He is revered for his landscapes. One well known painting being Wanderer Above the Sea of Fog.
