A Timeline of Art History

In art history there are many periods and movements that define how we view art today. Four important parts of art history include:

  1. Egyptian, Greek/Hellenistic, Roman
  2. Indian, Chinese, Japanese
  3. Renaissance
  4. Romanticism
Years Period Influential Artists
850 B.C.-500 A.D. Egyptian, Greek/Hellenistic, Roman
  • Myron (Greek sculptor)
  • Lysippus (Greek Sculptor)
653 B.C.-1900 A.D. Indian, Chinese, Japanese
  • Gu Kaizhi (Chinese painter, poet, and politician)
  • Hokusai (Japanese painter and printmaker)
1400-1600 Renaissance
  • Michelangelo (Italian sculptor and painter)
  • Raphael (Italian painter)
  • Albrecht Dürer (German painter)
1800-1850 Romanticism
  • William Blake (English poet and painter)
  • Caspar David Friedrich (German painter)
