Michelangelo

Michelangelo di Lodovico Buonarroti Simoni (1475-1564) was an Italian painter and sculptor. He is well-known for his work on the Sistine Chapel and his famous sculpture David.

Raphael

Raffaello Sanzio da Urbino (1483-1520) was an Italian painter. His famous works include The School of Athens and Transfiguration.
